HOW TO PLAY:


Two players roll a dice in a turn-based style game.

Rolling 2-6 adds the respective number to your 'turn total'.

Rolling a 1 resets your 'turn total' and switches to the other player's turn.

After rolling at least one time, a number 2-6, you may choose to 'hold' your 'turn total'.

This will deposit your 'turn total' into your 'current points', reset your 'turn total' to zero, and switch turns.

The goal is to get to 100 'current points' before the other player does!


Enjoy!
